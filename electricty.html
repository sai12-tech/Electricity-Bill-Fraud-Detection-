About The Project

PowerGuard AI is an intelligent electricity fraud detection system that helps electricity distribution companies identify abnormal power consumption patterns, meter tampering, illegal connections, and fake electricity bills. The system analyzes customer usage history using AI algorithms and instantly flags suspicious activities. It helps reduce financial losses, improve billing accuracy, and ensure fair electricity distribution.
